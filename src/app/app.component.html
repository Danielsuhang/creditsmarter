Financial Risks

Overspending


The easy availability of credit can often be dangerous to those who have a habit of impulsive purchasing items they cannot afford.

  • Credit score can be lowered when the cardholder spending reaches too close to their spending limit.
  • Accumulation of debt by not paying off monthly statement in full or at all. High APR can possibly leave the cardholder in debt FOREVER. Not even joking. The average APR for people with GOOD credit is almost 20% annually.
